Privacy Policy

Effective June 28, 2026

Summit Church School (“Summit,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates summitchurchschool.org and the Summit Family Hub online portal for enrolled homeschool families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and Family Hub.

Who this applies to

This policy applies to visitors to our public website, families applying for or enrolled in Summit Church School, and approved users of the Summit Family Hub. The Family Hub is intended for currently enrolled families and authorized staff only.

Information we collect

  • Contact and account information — such as your name, email address, and login credentials when you register for the Family Hub or contact us through our website.
  • Google sign-in information — if you choose “Sign in with Google,” we receive basic profile information from Google (such as your name and email address) through our authentication provider.
  • Enrollment and family records — information you submit through enrollment forms, the Family Hub, or directly to school staff, including documents related to your family’s church school enrollment.
  • Uploaded documents — files you or staff upload to the Family Hub, which may include government identification and other school-related documents assigned to your household.
  • Website usage data — basic technical information such as browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected through normal website operation and our hosting providers.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review enrollment applications and manage church school records
  • Provide and secure access to the Summit Family Hub
  • Share documents, tasks, and school resources with enrolled families
  • Send service-related emails, such as account confirmation, pending-approval notices, password reset messages, and approval notifications
  • Respond to questions submitted through our contact form
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate our services, including with:

  • Service providers that help us host the website, authenticate users, send email, process forms, or store documents securely (for example, hosting, database, email, and form providers).
  • Summit Church School staff and authorized administrators who need access to review signups, approve accounts, and manage family documents.
  • Legal or safety requirements when we believe disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect our families, staff, or services.

Data retention and security

We keep information only as long as needed for enrollment, church school operations, legal obligations, and legitimate school purposes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information, including encrypted connections and access controls on the Family Hub. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Children’s information

Summit Church School serves homeschooling families. Information about students is generally provided by parents or guardians in connection with enrollment and school records. The Family Hub is not intended for direct use by children without parental involvement.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of account information where applicable. You may choose email/password registration instead of Google sign-in. If you use Google sign-in, you can also manage certain permissions through your Google account settings.

Third-party links and services

Our website may link to third-party services such as enrollment forms, community groups, or payment tools. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them before submitting information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective” date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. Continued use of our website or Family Hub after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices may be sent to info@summitchurchschool.org or through the contact form on our main site.
