Application received

Thank you for completing your enrollment application. Our team has received your submission and will review it carefully.

What happens next

Here is the path from application to your Family Hub account. You do not need to take further action until we contact you.

Application submitted

Your secure online application is on file. If you saved progress earlier, your final submission is now complete.

2

Application review

Summit staff review each family’s application. We will email you once a decision is ready—before tuition payment is due.

3

Pay tuition after approval

Once approved, submit annual tuition on our website using PayPal or Cash App. View tuition options

Include the enrolling parent’s full name and email address in the payment note so we can match your payment to your application.

4

Create your Family Hub account

After approval, register for the Summit Family Hub to access documents, assigned tasks, and school resources. Create account — staff will approve access after your enrollment is confirmed.

5

First-year families

New families will be asked to provide a clear photo of a valid driver’s license or government-issued photo ID through the Family Hub. Your official enrollment letter will be sent by email or hard copy as arranged with our team.

Questions while you wait? We are happy to help.
