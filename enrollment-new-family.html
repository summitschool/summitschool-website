What to expect

Ryan will walk you through these steps personally. Here is the general path new families follow:

Application submitted

Your secure online application is on file.

2

Connect with Ryan

Reach out by Facebook, text, or email so we can answer questions and confirm next steps for your family.

3

Application review & tuition

We review your application and let you know when it is time to submit annual tuition.

4

Summit Family Hub signup

Every enrolled family uses the Family Hub for grade submissions, resources, assigned tasks, and accountability. Ryan will help you get set up when the time is right.

5

Welcome to Summit

Once enrollment is approved and Hub access is granted, your official enrollment documents and school-year resources will be available to your family.
